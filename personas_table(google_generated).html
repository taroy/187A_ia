Ethan Moser is 22 year old. He was born and raised in Aberdeen, South Dakota and is currently an undergraduate student at University of Louisiana, Lafayette. He is with the College of Education in their Secondary Education, General Science program. He is now on his junior year.

Ethan is also a linebacker on the Ragin’ Cajuns football team. Football was his main reason for choosing UL Lafayette. Sports is his passion and takes up a lot of his spear time. He wants a future as an NFL football player , but he is aware of the hard competition and has the teacher career as a back-up plan. If he gets any football related offers he is prepared to move from Lafayette to pursue his dream.

Scenario 1

Scenario context

The football season is over and Ethan has got to catch up on his academics. He is enrolled in PHYS 207, Introduction to Physics I, and needs to know when the next lecture is scheduled.

Scenario question

When is the next lecture in PHYS 207, Introduction to Physics I?

User task

Find the class schedule.

Steps

1. go to louisiana.edu

2. click on ‘current students’ menu

3. find and click on the link ‘schedule of classes’

4. find and click on the link ‘proceed to real time view’

5. select subject from the drop-down menu

6. type in course number

7. select term from the drop-down menu

8. click on the ‘find sections now’ button

9. find the section he is enrolled in

10. view the begin-end time of that section
